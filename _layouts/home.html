Our Team

We are a team of passionate students from Ottawa, the capital of the Great White North. We are driven by a deep fascination with robotics. What we do is explore the vast world of technology, where we can push the boundaries of our potential altogether.

In mid-2024, we embarked on this journey with the goal of competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge, one of the most prestigious robotics competitions in the world. Although we are still in the early stages, our vision is clear: to build and program a groundbreaking robot that will demonstrate our technical prowess >:).

Our Foundation

Our foundation is built on the principles of first-principle thinking, where we break down complex problems into their most basic elements and reassemble them in novel ways. This approach not only strengthens our understanding but also fuels our creativity and problem-solving skills.
